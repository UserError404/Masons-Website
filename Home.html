The Mason Bradley Holmberg Foundation

The Mason Bradley Holmberg Foundation is a nonprofit, charitable, and educational organization to support and educate individuals and their families from overcoming their addiction with drugs.

Help us end the suffering inflicted on millions of americans by opioid addiction

In 2020 there were 68,630 opioid related deaths... that number has risen to 80,411 in 2021 alone

75% of all overdose deaths in 2020 involved an opioid

The United States makes up 4.4% of the world's population, and consumes over 80% of the world's opioids

54% of more than 200,000 physicians received a payment from pharmaceutical companies that make opioids

1 kilogram of fentanyl has the potential to kill 500,000 people

Our Mission

It is our mission to bring awareness to the clear and present danger of Fentanyl and other drug- related addictions in our community’s youth.
We intend to aid and educate others by providing the resources necessary for individuals struggling with addiction to overcome their personal battles.
We also intend to provide additional knowledge and assistance to the families of those affected.

Our Goals

  • To provide comprehensive educational resources to individuals and families affected by addiction.
  • To bring awareness to the present dangers of drug-related dependency in today’s youth.
  • To provide the tools and resources necessary to guide and aid young individuals in overcoming drug addiction.
  • To connect individuals and families to their community through obtaining resources needed to overcome drug dependency.
  • To provide skills and resources necessary to prevent young individuals from entering a dependent lifestyle.
